about

I am a programmer primarily interested in static binary analysis.

I am the author of rdis, a binary analysis tool for linux.

I am a member of rainbowsandpwnies and an avid fan of the hacking team at the United States Military Academy at West Point.

I sometimes go by the name rednovae, which is an anagram of the name endeavor.

You can find more about me on linkedin, twitter and github.
